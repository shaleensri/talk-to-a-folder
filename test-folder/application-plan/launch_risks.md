# Launch Risks: Neighborhood Pantry Mobile App

## Risk: Low Volunteer Adoption
Volunteers may keep using text messages instead of the shift calendar. Mitigation: send calendar links in existing SMS reminders and ask coordinators to confirm shifts only through the app during the pilot.

## Risk: Appointment Confusion
Families may arrive outside their pickup windows if instructions are unclear. Mitigation: use plain-language confirmation messages, include translated pickup notes, and print appointment cards during the first month.

## Risk: Inventory Counts Become Stale
Inventory alerts only work if staff update counts regularly. Mitigation: add a daily closing checklist and make low-stock categories editable from the dashboard.

## Risk: Privacy Concerns
The app handles sensitive household information. Mitigation: keep family notes coordinator-only, audit exported reports, and avoid showing eligibility details to volunteers.
