# Biology 10 Lab Safety Policy

Students must follow safety directions at all times. Lab work may include glassware, prepared slides, food-coloring models, heat sources, and preserved specimens.

## Required Routines
- Wear goggles when instructed.
- Tie back long hair during wet labs and heat activities.
- Keep food and drinks away from lab tables.
- Report spills, broken glass, and injuries immediately.
- Wash hands after labs involving specimens, soil, or chemical indicators.

## Consequences
Unsafe behavior results in removal from the lab activity and completion of an alternate written assignment. Repeated unsafe behavior requires a family conference before the student returns to lab work.

## Make-Up Labs
Students who miss a lab may complete a data analysis version of the activity or attend the scheduled make-up session when materials are available.
