Weekly Readings: Biology 10

Week 1: What makes a question scientific?
Read pages 4-18 in the course packet and annotate examples of testable and non-testable questions.

Week 2: Cells as systems
Read "Inside the Cell" and identify the function of the nucleus, mitochondria, ribosomes, cell membrane, and chloroplasts.

Week 3: Energy transformations
Read the photosynthesis and cellular respiration overview. Create a two-column comparison chart.

Week 4: DNA and inheritance
Read the Mendel case study and complete the vocabulary notes for allele, genotype, phenotype, dominant, and recessive.

Week 5: Evolution evidence
Read the fossil record excerpt and the antibiotic resistance article. Bring one discussion question to class.

Week 6: Ecosystem interactions
Read the food web case study and identify producers, consumers, decomposers, and keystone species.
