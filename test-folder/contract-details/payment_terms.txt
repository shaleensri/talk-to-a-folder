Payment Terms

Northstar Catering will invoice City Arts Center within five business days after each event. Payment is due thirty calendar days from the invoice date.

Deposit policy:
- Public events with more than 150 guests require a 25 percent deposit.
- Donor dinners require a 40 percent deposit due when the menu is approved.
- Internal staff events do not require deposits unless the estimated cost exceeds $5,000.

Late payment:
Invoices more than fifteen days overdue may accrue a 1.5 percent monthly finance charge. Northstar may pause new event bookings if two or more invoices are overdue.

Reimbursable expenses:
Special rentals, security-required delivery windows, and custom printed menus must be pre-approved in writing. Approved expenses are billed at cost with receipts attached.
