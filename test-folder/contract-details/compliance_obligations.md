# Compliance Obligations

## Food Safety
Northstar Catering must maintain all required food handler certifications and comply with local health department rules. Temperature logs are required for events lasting more than three hours.

## Insurance
Northstar must maintain general liability coverage of at least $2,000,000 aggregate and workers compensation coverage as required by law. Updated certificates must be delivered before the agreement start date and after policy renewal.

## Accessibility
City Arts Center may request ingredient labels, allergen cards, and alternate service layouts for accessibility needs. Northstar must make reasonable accommodations when requests are received at least seven business days before the event.

## Confidentiality
Donor guest lists, event budgets, and private event notes may not be shared outside the catering team. Printed materials containing guest details must be returned to City Arts Center or securely destroyed after each event.
